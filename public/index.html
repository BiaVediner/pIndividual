In Schrödinger's mental experiment, a cat is trapped in a box isolated from everything that exists, inside the box there is also a radiation meter and a sample of , if it decays it triggers a mechanism that will kill the cat. This material is in a state of superposition and therefore the cat is also in a state of superposition alive and dead. The moment we open the box, the superposition ends, and we see the cat alive or dead.

This experiment is more a reflection than an explanation, it demonstrates that there are limitations to our knowledge and perhaps our representation of reality does not represent the real world.
