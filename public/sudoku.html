How to play Sudoku

  • Select an empty cell and choose a number from 1 to 9 to fill it;
  • The objective is to fill all the cells following these three basic rules:
    • Don't repeat the numbers in the same row;
    • Don't repeat the numbers in the same column;
    • Don't repeat the numbers in the same subgrid (3x3 box).
